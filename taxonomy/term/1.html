Setting it up on your site

FreeRangeFeeedback is really simple to setup on your site.

1. Get an account

To get started register for an account.

2. Cut and paste

Once you've registered, FreeRangeFeeedback creates a piece of code for you to embed into your website. You can find it in My account under My Sites. You can simply cut-and-paste that code into your site.

3. Get feedback

By pasting the code into your site template (in the header/footer or perhaps a sidebar), you empower your users to leave feedback relevant to that page.

Leaving feedback

The process of leaving feedback is simple. Clicking the Feedback link opens a comment on the page, with a textbox for you to type your feedback into.

This gives the comment a title, but there's a lot more you can do with it.

Moving
Click and drag the titlebar or the body of the comment to move it around the page.

Adding posts
Click the 'Add' button to leave more detailed feedback. These posts can be added or deleted at any point.
