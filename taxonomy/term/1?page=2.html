Play with the technology

Click Feedback below to see how easy it is for users of this site to leave feedback.

  • Feedback

The Feedback link will place a yellow note on the page in 'edit' mode. Simply type a message to write it into the note's headline field. When you're done, press return or click outside your note. At that point your note is saved. Hover anywhere over your note to see more options; you can drag is around the page, resize or edit it.
